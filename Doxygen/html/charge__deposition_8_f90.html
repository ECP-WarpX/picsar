Order 1 3D vector charge deposition routine.

Computes charge density on grid vectorized at order 1 (HV-SCHEME v2). This routine does vectorize on SIMD architecture with good performances: Speedup>2 on AVX 256 bits. The parameter lvect is the vector length and is originally at 64 for order 1.
